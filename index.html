Swiss Excellence

Registered in Basel's trade register since September 2023, operating at the heart of Europe's leading life sciences corridor with world-class institutional support.

Noor Biotech GmbH is an innovative Basel-based biotech start-up co-founded by Saudi Arabian scientist and inventor Nuha Alekhmini. The company specializes in developing a novel, rapid, and cost-effective biosensor technology platform for the early detection of inflammatory and infectious diseases.

Supported by Basel Area Business & Innovation, the company has ongoing plans to commercialize its biosensor platforms and scale its team to up to 40 employees — reinforcing Basel's position as a global hub for life sciences innovation.

Clinical Focus

Targeting the
Most Critical Conditions

Cellular Detection
Pathogen Identification

Sepsis

A life-threatening condition caused by the body's extreme response to infection. Early detection is critical — sepsis mortality rises by up to 7% with every hour of delayed treatment.

11M+ deaths per year globally
Early detection saves lives

Acute Lung Injury

ALI and ARDS represent severe inflammatory conditions of the lungs with high mortality. Early biomarker identification enables targeted intervention before irreversible damage occurs.

30–40% mortality rate in severe cases
Precision monitoring

Inflammatory Diseases

Chronic and acute inflammatory conditions underlie the majority of severe diseases. Our biosensor platform detects key cytokines and inflammation markers with unprecedented speed.

50%+ of all disease burden
Broad-spectrum detection
